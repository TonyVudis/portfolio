TRANSFORMING TYPOGRAPHY

October 2025

This assignment focuses on creating a unique typographic composition by integrating and combining an assigned letter, number, or symbol with another. The goal was to craft a new design through the manipulation of each form while maintaining a balanced relationship between the two. It's essential to perserve teh readability and integrity of each element, avoiding any dominance of one form over the other.

Process:

Tasked with drawing 100 different iterations of the assigned letter forms.

Iterations:

After working through the letter combinations of E and M as well as E and B, I found that E and B had more potential to fit the requirements.

Final Iterations:

I picked the iteration design that I did because it felt the most unique and neither the B or the E felt too dominating in that space. But we first had to narrow it down to 10 digital iterations and then further experiment with the layout and design.
