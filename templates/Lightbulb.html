OBJECT TRANSLATION

September 2025

This project was to focus on the ability to see, analyze, and draw abstract objects/images which is vitally importsnt and necessary skill in visual communication design. It is the cornerstone of the design and development of graphic imagery, such as trademark/logos/logotypes, symbols, and signs.

Referances:

Process:

We had to choose from 3 everyday objects and draw them 3 different times from different perspectives. Between a mousepad, sunglasses and a stained glass lightbulb, I chose the lightbulb. I wanted to explore all the different ways you could play with reflection and metal material by showing them in a simplifed design.

Iterations:

For my different iterations, I explored different background colors. I was experimenting with the black background because I thought it could be interesting like a lightbulb in the dark illuminating the space. But after feedback I was told it took away from the simplified design. So in return I added more lines to help relay the shape and lighting of the lightbulb to better render the design.
